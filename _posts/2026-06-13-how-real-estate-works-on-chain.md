### What Fractional Ownership Really Means (Without the Jargon)

**The simplest explanation you’ll ever hear.**

Imagine you and nine friends want to buy a ₦5 million car.

**Traditional route:**\
One person pays ₦5M → One person owns it.

**Fractional route:**\
All ten people pay ₦500,000 each → Everyone owns 10%.

Now apply this to real estate:

**A ₦500 million building becomes 500,000 tokens worth ₦1,000 each.**

1 token = 0.0002% ownership

100 tokens = 0.02%

10,000 tokens = 2%

If the building generates **₦20 million yearly rent,** then:

• Own 100 tokens = Earn ₦4,000 yearly. Automatically.

Not through agents.\
Not through paperwork.\
Not through banks.

But through smart contracts digital rules that execute themselves.

### How Real Estate Gets Tokenized: The 5-Step Breakdown

Making the technical simple.

1. **Put the building inside a legal container (SPE/LLC)**

The property goes into a company

Tokens represent shares of that company, not random “crypto promises.”

Example:\
RealT uses an LLC for every property and issues ERC-20 RealTokens representing shares.

2. **Value the property & divide ownership into tokens**

Example tokenization structure:

* Property value: $1,000,000
* Tokens created: 1,000,000
* Each token: $1
* Each token = 0.0001% ownership

**Simple. Transparent. Regulated.**

3. **Investors buy tokens via a regulated offering**

Through:

* USDT, USDC, ETH
* Bank transfer
* local on-ramps

Tokens appear in investor wallets instantly.

4. **Smart contracts automate everything**

Smart contracts handle:

* Ownership records
* Monthly rental income
* Transfer of tokens
* KYC + restrictions
* Governance rules

Example:\
If you own 1000 tokens, and the building produces ₦2M rental income this month:

* You automatically get ₦4000.

**No bank\
No agent\
No delays.**\
5. **Secondary market trading (the liquidity breakthrough)**

Traditional property:

**3 - 6 months to sell**

Tokenized property:

**30 - 60 seconds to sell your fractions**

Early data shows tokenized property trades at 15% monthly turnover, meaning investors actually move in and out freely something impossible before.

**REITs** = You own shares in a company.

**Crowdfunding** = Locked capital for years.

“But Is It Real Ownership?” **Yes, Here’s Why**

When you hold property tokens, you legally own:

* Shares of the SPE
* Claim to rental income
* Claim to appreciation
* Governance rights

**You don’t own:**

* Physical keys
* Personal occupancy rights

It works exactly like owning shares of Apple but here, the underlying asset is real estate.
